Financial Tracker - Privacy Policy

Welcome to Financial Tracker ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Financial Tracker collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the Financial Tracker mobile application ("App"). This privacy policy applies to the App and all products and services offered by Financial Tracker.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you choose to log in with Firebase email and password, we collect the email address associated with your account. If you opt to store data on Firebase Firestore, the following data may be collected:

  • Email and password for authentication.
  • User-generated financial transactions manually created by the user.
  • User preferences, including but not limited to budget start day, budget limits, and transaction categories.

b. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information such as device information, usage statistics, and crash reports to improve app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

Personal Information:

Personal information is used to identify you and provide personalized services. User-generated financial transactions are utilized to create dashboards, charts, and support budgeting on the user's mobile device. This information is not shared with third parties except as outlined in this policy.

Non-Personal Information:

Non-personal information is used for analytics, app improvement, and to enhance the user experience.

4. Firebase and Google Services

a. Firebase

If you choose to log in with Firebase, your data will be stored securely on Firebase servers. Firebase follows its own privacy policy, which you can review here.

b. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how our app is used. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to analyze and report on user interactions. The information generated is transmitted to Google servers and stored in accordance with Google's privacy practices. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

c. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob for displaying ads in our app. Google AdMob collects and processes personal data in accordance with its privacy policy. For more information, please review Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

Data stored on Firebase Firestore can be deleted upon user request. Locally stored data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

6. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on our website and within the app.

10. Consent
