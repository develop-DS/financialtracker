Kasboek - Support & FAQ

Last updated: February 2026

1. How do I add a transaction?

Open Home and tap the blue + button. Select income or expense, fill in the amount and category, then save.

2. How do I edit or remove a transaction?

Open a transaction from a list (Home, Transactions, or category details), make your changes, and save. To remove it, use the delete action on the transaction screen.

3. How do budget cycles work?

A cycle is your monthly budget window based on your selected start day. Home and Insights show values for the selected cycle in the header date range.

4. How do I update budgets and categories?

Go to Home and tap Edit Budget to update limits. For category management, open Settings and tap Edit All Categories.

5. Why are Insights locked?

Insights are unlocked by watching a short rewarded video. Initial unlock is 48 hours. Each extension adds 72 hours.

6. Can I use the app offline?

Yes. You can continue adding and editing data offline. Sync continues when your connection is available again.

7. How do I change language, currency, or theme?

Open Settings to change app language, currency symbol, and appearance (Light, Dark, or System).

8. How does account sign-in and backup work?

You can sign in from Settings to use cloud backup. Local data can also be exported as JSON backup and transactions as CSV.
